Clean Gem Promise

Every item is reviewed, cleaned when appropriate, organized, fairly priced, and presented with dignity. Items that are unsafe, heavily stained, smoky, moldy, broken, or not ready for resale are not accepted into the Treasured Twice selling system.

Reviewed

Items are checked for condition, safety, wear, missing pieces, odors, and resale readiness.

Prepared

Items are cleaned when appropriate, organized by section, and presented with dignity.

Fair-priced

Pricing is designed to stay affordable for families while supporting a trustworthy local resale platform.
