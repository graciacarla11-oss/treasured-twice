Donations

🤝

Donation intake

Treasured Twice will review donations for cleanliness, condition, seasonality, safety, and resale fit. Donation scheduling is a future feature; for now, this page is a placeholder for intake guidance.

Accepted

  • Clean clothing for women, men, kids, and baby
  • Accessories, shoes, and small home goods
  • Hidden gems in safe, sellable condition
✓

Not accepted

  • Recalled, unsafe, stained, wet, or heavily damaged items
  • Items with strong odors, pests, or missing essential parts
  • Large furniture, hazardous materials, or expired products
