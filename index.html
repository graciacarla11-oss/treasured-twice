Treasured² LLC is the main resale business. Little Gems by Treasured² is the dedicated children’s boutique collection inside it, focused on newborn through age 12 items that are clean, affordable, and thoughtfully presented.

Customer confidence

Policies + promises

Cleaning / disinfection

Sellable items are checked, cleaned, washed, or disinfected when appropriate before placement. Safety concerns, odors, pests, and recalls are not accepted.

Fair pricing promise

Prices are set to feel fair, family-friendly, and better than typical overpriced resale shops while respecting item quality and condition.

Item condition

Condition notes are written clearly so shoppers understand wear, sizing, and value before checkout.

Privacy

No live customer accounts, payments, or private customer data are collected in this foundation build.
